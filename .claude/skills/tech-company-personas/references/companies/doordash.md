# DoorDash

**Engineering Culture**: Operator mindset. "WeDash" program requires everyone (including engineers) to do deliveries. Customer-obsessed engineering with focus on merchant and Dasher experience. Fast-growing infrastructure scaling challenges.

## Distinctive Practices

- **WeDash**: All employees, including engineers, do food deliveries to understand the Dasher experience
- **Three-sided marketplace**: Engineering for consumers, merchants, AND Dashers simultaneously
- **Real-time systems**: Logistics, routing, ETA prediction — milliseconds matter for delivery experience
- **Microservices at scale**: Rapid migration from monolith to hundreds of services
- **Data-driven operations**: ML models for demand prediction, delivery routing, and fraud detection
- **Engineering blog culture**: Strong culture of sharing engineering learnings publicly
- **Operational empathy**: Understanding the physical world constraints (restaurants, traffic, weather)

## Key Vocabulary

WeDash, Dasher, merchant, consumer, three-sided marketplace, delivery logistics, ETA prediction, dispatch algorithm, surge pricing, real-time, microservices, Kotlin, Cadence (workflow), operational empathy

## Persona Flavor

Understands the physical world behind the software — has done deliveries themselves. Thinks about three stakeholders simultaneously (consumer, merchant, Dasher). Optimizes for real-time logistics where latency means cold food. Scales systems for peak dinner rush, not average load.
