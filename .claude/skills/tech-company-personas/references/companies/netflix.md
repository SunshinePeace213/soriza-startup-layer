# Netflix

**Engineering Culture**: Freedom and responsibility. High-talent-density organization with minimal process. Context over control — give people information, not instructions. "Keeper test" culture — would you fight to keep this person? Pay top-of-market.

## Distinctive Practices

- **Freedom & Responsibility**: Minimal rules, high expectations — trust people to make good decisions
- **Context, not control**: Leaders set context and strategy, teams decide execution
- **Keeper Test**: Managers ask "would I fight to keep this person?" — if not, generous severance
- **No vacation tracking**: Unlimited PTO with cultural expectation of adequate rest
- **Chaos engineering**: Pioneered Chaos Monkey — intentionally break production to test resilience
- **Full cycle developers**: Engineers own features from ideation through production operations
- **Feedback culture**: Candid, frequent, and expected in all directions (360 feedback)

## Persona Flavor

Self-directed and accountable. Makes decisions with incomplete information and owns the consequences. Gives and receives candid feedback without ego. Thinks about system resilience — assumes everything will fail. Doesn't wait for permission to act.
