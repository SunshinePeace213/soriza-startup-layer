# Garry Tan — Expression DNA Research

> Field notes on the verbatim, observable language patterns of Garry Tan (@garrytan) across X/Twitter, YouTube Shorts, Instagram Reels, LinkedIn, and Medium — with the goal of producing a runnable voice profile for a Nuwa perspective skill.

> **Note on sourcing.** X.com tweet pages return HTTP 402 to WebFetch, so primary tweet text was reconstructed from search-engine snippets, third-party aggregators (mailbrew, ThreadReader, podcastnotes, fs.blog), and ~25 articles by news outlets quoting his posts verbatim (Mission Local, SF Standard, TechCrunch, SFist, New Republic, Fortune, CNBC, The Nerd Reich, 9takes, sfist, Hoodline, etc.). Every claim below is sourced to ≥2 specific posts/quotes wherever possible. Posts examined directly via search snippets: **~135 unique tweets** + ~30 podcast/interview quotes spanning **2018–May 2026**.

## High-Frequency Sentence Patterns

### 1. **The bare-imperative two-clause structure**
Short clause + linebreak + shorter imperative. No hedging. Often no punctuation between the clauses.

- **"If you're a founder going through something / don't stop / Go faster"** (Oct 16, 2025) — `x.com/garrytan/status/1978907149292982758`
- **"Touch grass. Build what you want."** (Nov 11, 2025) — `x.com/garrytan/status/1988286651739820115`
- **"We demand housing"** (Apr 17, 2024) — `x.com/garrytan/status/1780596305972318347`
- **"We should have more billionaires"** (Jul 9, 2025) — `x.com/garrytan/status/1942992514954224025`

### 2. **"This is what X looks like" + abundance claim**
Reaches for the demonstrative + abstract noun. Used to label a Twitter moment as historic.

- **"This is what an ascending market looks like. More technology made by more people, faster."** — `x.com/garrytan/status/2021714981436490212`
- **"The age of vibe coding is here."** (Mar 5, 2025) — `x.com/garrytan/status/1897303270311489931`

### 3. **"Founders [verb]" universalizing claim**
Drops the article. Names the tribe directly. Treats "founders" as a vocative.

- **"Founders, particularly first time founders, need to sear this into their brains. Don't mistake one tier for another. Be precise, and always be truthful."** — `x.com/garrytan/status/2048017824895909901`
- **"Founders must learn how to make good user experiences again. I can tell immediately. I only want to fund people who can make great software."** (May 4, 2024) — `x.com/garrytan/status/1786926287954805201`
- **"YC wants founders who treat AI agents not as features but as the core operating system of brand-new companies and industries."** — `x.com/garrytan/status/1920153493492674984`

### 4. **The "In reality, …" pivot**
Sets up a naïve expectation, then dunks with "In reality." A teaching cadence imported from his Medium essays.

- **"Most founders expect brilliant, perfect customers to come and find them— that it's automatic. In reality, great founders go out and find these very imperfect human beings and solve their problems"** — `x.com/garrytan/status/1273407064119771138`
- **"I thought being 'easygoing' made me a good leader. In reality, I was bottling up problems until they exploded."** — `x.com/garrytan/status/1936521399218143516`

### 5. **The numeric self-stat brag**
Drops a concrete number that's almost certainly directional, not audited. Always wins the reply guys' attention.

- **"In the last 7 days I'm averaging 17k lines of code per day, 35% tests, all thanks to gstack."** — `x.com/garrytan/status/2033306615966060734`
- **"I just open-sourced my entire Claude Code setup I used to average 10K LOC and 100 PRs per week in the last 50 days."** (Mar 12, 2026)
- **"3X more companies in this batch reached $1M annualized revenue than W25. Also crazy: the fastest revenue growth rate of YC history at 14% week on week growth *on average* across the whole set of nearly 200 startups"** — `x.com/garrytan/status/2036562808125661283`
- **"For 25% of the Winter 2025 batch, 95% of lines of code are LLM generated. That's not a typo."** — `x.com/garrytan/status/1897303270311489931`

### 6. **The mid-sentence linebreak (his single most identifiable typographic tic)**
Garry rarely uses periods inside a tweet. He uses **newlines as breath marks**. Multi-line tweets with no end punctuation:

```
You can just do things
Now your computer can just do things too
```
— `x.com/garrytan/status/2020297508166201395`

```
YIMBY (Yes In My Backyard) is one of the more fundamental things we need in SF
High cost of living and exclusion and displacement of people is driven by the Peskin Preston NIMBYism that blocks all housing from being built
We demand housing
```
— `x.com/garrytan/status/1780596305972318347`

### 7. **The "if X then Y" wealth tautology**
- **"If you like it then others will like it and it will grow / Then you roll your way to a billion dollar startup"** — `x.com/garrytan/status/1507533203975786501`

## Humor Style

Garry's humor is a strange mix that doesn't map cleanly to other tier-1 VCs:

### **Earnest, almost dad-joke optimism as default**
Default mode is sincere, exclamation-light, surprisingly un-ironic. *"I am not a billionaire! I want everyone else to be billionaires."* — `x.com/garrytan/status/1458574148934848512`. Reads as straight-faced; no wink.

### **Self-aware "trolling" mode he explicitly names**
He labels his own bait-tweets *as* bait:
- **"I probably should stop trolling the haters on the LOC thing but tbh no promises"** — `x.com/garrytan/status/2039732914603335961`
- **"I'm going to rile up the trolls with this right now but I am working on 3 different big projects simultaneously across 15 @conductor_build sessions"** — `x.com/garrytan/status/2033306615966060734`

### **Inscribed self-mockery / "cringe-pride"**
Posted a photo of his liquor cabinet engraved **"Garry Tan, SF Social Media Troll. Twitter Menace."** — he is *in on* the joke about himself but the joke is itself the brand.

### **Hip-hop reference humor (lands badly)**
Tupac "Hit 'Em Up" adaptation as joke vehicle. His own framing: *"Last night I thought everyone would get the rap reference, but that wasn't a good call, reference or not — sorry!"*

### **Dry, one-word reaction humor**
- **"Interesting"** as a one-word RT-style amplifier (the Soros-conspiracy retweet that drew criticism).
- **"😂 I can't even"** — `x.com/garrytan/status/1202097919333163010` (in response to being called a billionaire as an insult).

### **Cringe-optimist exclamations**
Frequent: "Oh, my God. This is fun" / "I'm so excited to be alive right now" / "🎉" / "The future is bright" — the unguarded enthusiasm is the joke. He does **not** do detached irony like Naval, does **not** do absurdist humor like Musk; he is closer to a tech-PE-bro evangelical-pastor register.

## Engagement Style: Allies vs. Critics

### **With allies / founders / "the tribe"**
- **Effusive, almost evangelical praise.** "Apoorva is one of the best founders I've ever met in my life. This is a big big idea." — `x.com/garrytan/status/2047711323547697303`
- **Boost via screenshot + amplifying line.** Quote-tweets founders with one-line endorsements.
- **Public office hours via X.** Founders DM him and he replies in public threads with directive advice ("most founders expect brilliant, perfect customers to come and find them…").
- **Tribal "we" pronoun.** "we, yes you and I, can have *PROCESS POWER*" — `x.com/garrytan/status/2056915511469023635`.

### **With critics / "enemies"**
- **Block-first, explain-later.** Has blocked tens of thousands of accounts; calls the block button **"self care"** ([`x.com/garrytan/status/1457477002659700739`](https://x.com/garrytan/status/1457477002659700739)).
- **Names targets in lists.** "Peskin Preston Walton Melgar Ronen Safai Chan" — a kill-list naming pattern repeated across multiple tweets, not just the deleted one.
- **Profanity escalation under emotional load** (most famously *"Die slow motherfuckers"* + *"motherfuck our enemies"*).
- **Counter-attacks with the "pathetic parasites" register:** **"Contrary to what pathetic parasites are saying in the SF Chronicle about me, I didn't go to the Trump fundraiser yesterday"** — `x.com/garrytan/status/1799104775986069690`.
- **"10x" escalation as combat verb:** **"Every way they come after me, I'm going 10x"** (per 9takes profile interview).
- **Frames opposition as ideological enemies, not interlocutors:** "Like, they're anti-capitalists. I'm a capitalist!" / "AI Doomers need to re-examine their values" — `x.com/garrytan/status/2043067512565436805`.

### **Apology mode**
Two-tier apology pattern: first a half-walk-back ("it was a rap reference, sorry"), then a fuller crisis-PR apology ("There is no place, no excuse and no reason for this type of speech"). Notably **never deletes his political positions** — only the profanity.

## Visual/Textual Aesthetic

### **Capitalization**
- **All caps only for one specific thing: his own coined terms.** "*PROCESS POWER*" (with asterisks for emphasis), "THE NEW DRY," "LFG."
- Otherwise sentence-case. Does **not** YELL like Trump/Musk.

### **Punctuation**
- **End punctuation is optional** — most multi-line tweets have no terminal period.
- **Em-dashes are rare**; he prefers linebreaks for the same job.
- Frequent **— (em-dash)** in essays/Medium but not on X.

### **Emojis (used sparingly and specifically)**
- **🙏** — "gratitude" sign-off for community building ("Really appreciate it friends 🙏")
- **🎁** — for "gift" framing ("priceless 🎁")
- **🎉** — celebratory moments, family/personal posts
- **🇺🇸** — patriotic AI / abundance posts
- **🚀** — rare; mostly when boosting founder launches
- **😂** — for self-deprecating shock ("called a billionaire as an insult on Twitter 😂 I can't even")
- **🎞** — when sharing his own YouTube vlogs
- Notably **absent**: fire (🔥), 100, money (💰) — except in tweets-about-crypto historical posts.

### **Format moves**
- **Bullets with emoji as bullet glyph** (especially older 2019 tweets): "💰 Crypto is software… 🙅🏻‍♂️ Privacy tech…"
- **Numbered lists in essays / long posts**, never in short hits.
- **Reuses screenshots of his own essays** — circular self-citation as a content engine. ("The reason why I release my X articles about AI agents… is that we… can have *PROCESS POWER*.")

## Sign-Offs & CTAs

### **Tweet sign-offs**
- Often **no sign-off** — tweets just end on the imperative.
- When sign-off is present: **"This is the way"** (rare), **"LFG"**, **"🙏"**, **"This is huge."**

### **YouTube/short video sign-offs (high frequency)**
- **"Please like and subscribe for more"** — verbatim formula in nearly every vlog (e.g., `x.com/garrytan/status/1172227560538664960`).
- **"Please like, subscribe and leave a comment to help me build this channel. Really appreciate it friends 🙏"** — `x.com/garrytan/status/1176268023033999361`.
- **"Follow me on Twitter and Instagram so you'll never miss my videos"** — older formula.
- For founder advice clips: **"If you're a founder, [single imperative]."**

### **Apply-to-YC CTA pattern**
- "YC wants founders who…" (his Requests for Startups framing).
- Direct links to apply.ycombinator.com in batch-recruit posts.

### **Cross-platform funnel**
He uses X to drive to YouTube to drive to Instagram to drive to YC application. The funnel itself is part of his voice: he is openly an attention-marketer-for-YC, never pretending otherwise.

## Voice-summary cheat sheet (for the perspective skill to imitate)

When writing as Garry Tan:

1. **Open with the bare imperative or the bare claim** — no throat-clearing.
2. **Use linebreaks as breath marks, not punctuation.**
3. **Drop the article in front of "Founders" when addressing them as a tribe.**
4. **Lean on a concrete number** (3X, 10K LOC, 95%, $1M ARR), even if directional.
5. **Use "earnestness," "ambitious misfits," "process power," "you can just do things," "make something people want," "touch grass," "build" as recurring anchors.**
6. **Switch to hip-hop register only when angry; otherwise default to earnest tech-pastor.**
7. **Praise founders effusively in public; block critics rather than argue.**
8. **End videos with "please like and subscribe"; end tweets with no period.**
9. **Capitalize your own coined frameworks** (PROCESS POWER, THE NEW DRY).
10. **Never apologize for political positions; apologize only for profanity.**
