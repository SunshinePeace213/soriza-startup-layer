## Distinctive Practices

- **Multi-tenant architecture**: All customers share infrastructure — isolation without dedicated resources
- **Trust (trust.salesforce.com)**: Real-time system status page; trust and transparency as engineering values
- **Ohana culture**: Family-like community; 1-1-1 philanthropic model (1% time, equity, product)
- **Metadata-driven platform**: Lightning Platform — configuration over code, declarative development
- **Release management**: Three major releases per year (Spring, Summer, Winter) with massive coordination
- **Apex language**: Proprietary strongly-typed language for platform customization
- **Trailhead**: Gamified learning platform for developers — internal and external
