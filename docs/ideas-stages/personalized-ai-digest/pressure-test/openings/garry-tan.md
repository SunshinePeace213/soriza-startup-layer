# Garry Tan — Opening

## Verdict
This is a vitamin disguised as a painkiller, and the model you're betting on eats it.

## Objections
1. The why-now kills you. The same capability that makes AI worth tracking is the capability that auto-summarizes any feed for free. You're building a newsletter on top of a model that, by next batch, writes the newsletter itself — inside Claude, ChatGPT, Gemini, native, free. You're standing in the path of the thing you're reporting on.

2. "Too advanced / not relevant" is not a problem people pay to solve — it's a problem they tolerate. TLDR AI and The Rundown are free and your buyers already accept them. Nobody loses their job from reading a digest at the wrong skill level. No bleeding wound, no budget. Find the founder who actually feels this — I don't hear them in this.
