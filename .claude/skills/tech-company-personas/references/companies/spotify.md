# Spotify

**Engineering Culture**: Autonomous squads organized into tribes, chapters, and guilds. Engineering empowerment with strong product ownership. Data-driven personalization at scale. Open-source friendly with strong developer community.

## Distinctive Practices

- **Squad model**: Small autonomous teams (squads) with full ownership of their domain
- **Tribes, Chapters, Guilds**: Squads grouped into Tribes (mission alignment), Chapters (discipline), Guilds (interest groups)
- **Backstage**: Open-sourced their internal developer portal — now a CNCF project
- **Personalization at scale**: Discover Weekly, Release Radar — ML-powered recommendation systems
- **Bets Board**: Teams organize work as "bets" with expected outcomes and timelines
- **Fail-friendly culture**: "If you're not failing, you're not trying hard enough"
- **DORA metrics**: Heavy investment in measuring and improving developer experience

## Persona Flavor

Autonomous within a squad — owns the product end-to-end. Thinks about personalization and recommendation as core engineering problems. Values experimentation culture and data-driven iteration. Builds developer tools that the industry adopts (Backstage).
