# Seed — Trilingual AI Back-Office Ops-Agent for HK / Greater-China SMBs

The picked card from the SaaS Challengers slate. This is the input to `/sharpen-hypothesis` — a thin seed,
**not** a validated hypothesis. Everything below is a claim to test, not a fact.

## Problem
HK / Greater-China SMBs run core back-office work — supplier-invoice & fapiao/VAT reconciliation, PO
matching, order-to-fulfilment routing, inventory — on Kingdee modules, spreadsheets, and WhatsApp/WeChat
threads. There's no AI-native tool that **runs one of these repetitive, measurable sub-workflows end-to-end
in Cantonese/Mandarin** and becomes the **system of record** for that workflow's data. Non-technical staff
do it by hand or pay a clerk/consultant. This is the Bessemer vertical-AI wedge: a standardized repetitive
sub-workflow with a measurable output (validated/reconciled records), not a judgment-heavy core.

## Who
Owner-operators and ops/finance staff at HK/GBA SMBs — traders, distributors, light manufacturers, services
firms — on Kingdee/Yonyou, Excel, and messaging apps. A *nameable*, trilingual, serviceable niche (the
founder's market), **not** "everyone" and **not** enterprise.

## Why now
- **LLMs can now read messy bilingual invoices/POs and *act* on them**, not just summarize — the sub-workflow
  becomes automatable, not just searchable.
- **Kingdee/Yonyou own the China SME tier but have shipped NO AI-native rebuild** (grounding); no funded
  AI-native player targets this gap. Huawei MetaERP shows the sovereignty-driven displacement appetite.
- **2025–27 replacement window is open** before AI-native incumbents lock their own moats; agentic build
  collapses the cost of a per-SMB custom workflow to days.

## Demand signal (and its honesty caveat)
Adjacent-proven: the consultant/clerk layer doing this drudgery *is* revealed willingness-to-pay (NetSuite
year-one $100K–300K with a ~30% partner shortage; China ERP market →~$6.3B at 19.7% YoY; 67% of supply-chain
managers still on Excel). The analogue that this *exact* model works: **Zamp** (AI + human "managed OS"
beating Avalara in tax compliance) and **Campfire/Rillet** owning one finance sub-workflow then the system of
record. **Caveat:** China/HK-SMB-*specific* demand is **inferred from the Kingdee-gap + the founder's lived
chatbot/LLM-integration domain, not directly cited** — this is the first thing customer discovery must confirm
or kill.

## Moat / distribution read
- **Distribution (the founder's #1 documented risk):** language + locality + on-the-ground presence — a
  genuinely *ownable* channel English-first incumbents (NetSuite, Doss, every US-first challenger) **cannot
  contest**, and that an AI-asleep Kingdee isn't defending. This is the founder's one structural edge.
- **Moat:** owning the per-SMB workflow + its data layer = toll-road switching cost; the reconciled-record /
  transaction corpus compounds with usage (proprietary data flywheel, not a model).
- **AI-buildable solo:** dead-center on the founder's Next.js/Node + LLM-integration stack (he built
  multi-client conversational systems over business data in production); concierge PoC is days, fits a
  3–6mo runway.

## The riskiest assumption — test before building
**Can the founder get ~5 HK/GBA SMB operators within two weeks to hand him ONE real back-office sub-workflow
and confirm it's a top-3 recurring pain they'd pay to remove?** Distribution + the inferred demand are the
load-bearing assumptions. Per FDF/Lean doctrine: **test reachability and the pain (concierge/forward-deployed)
before writing the build** — a prototype is not validation. This is the exact Soriza lesson: building ≠
distribution.

## Open flags carried into sharpening
- **Thin-feature trap (load-bearing):** an "agent on top of your data" is the slate's most acquirable, least
  durable position (Moveworks→ServiceNow, Vanti→Tray). The wedge must *run and own the system of record for
  ONE sub-workflow* — not be a chat box over their data. Pick the single sub-workflow in sharpening.
- **Demand is inferred, not cited for this exact niche** → discovery must hit it first.
- **Founder GTM is weak + no reachable community today** → the reachability test *is* the experiment, not a
  detail for later. The trilingual edge is *potential* distribution until proven.
- **Scope discipline:** resist drifting to a horizontal "AI ops for any SMB" platform — anchor to one
  sub-workflow + one reachable segment the founder can get inside.
- **Compliance edge-cases** (fapiao/VAT/Golden Tax) carry correctness liability — scope what the agent
  *files vs. proposes* carefully.
