# Competitor Tiers — soriza-operator-os

*Facet provenance for `market-research.md`. Researcher: startup-idea-researcher (founder-blind). Context, not a kill.*

**Headline:** No single brand-safe product integrates persona-state vault + cross-modal consistency + cross-platform scheduling + disclosure automation today. The only named all-in-one (Glambase) is adult-skewed and dysfunctional; SynthLife is the closest brand-safe near-fit but is missing persona-state and disclosure. The window is real but closing — Picsart shipped Persona/Storyline (Mar 2026) and HeyGen ($100M ARR, ~1,024% YoY) is the most credible ceiling threat.

## Tier 1 — DIRECT (all-in-one AI-influencer / persona "operator" products)

- **Glambase** — persona creation + monetization dashboard, "passive-income virtual influencer." ~$15–50/mo or $347 one-time. **Adult-skewed in practice** despite ToS (Trustpilot: x-rated outputs, no age verification, reference-upload removed without notice, poor support). No cross-platform scheduling, no disclosure, no integrated voice/video pipeline; monetization = onsite companion-chat, not IG/TikTok publishing. The only named direct "all-in-one" is effectively an adult-GFE product.
- **SynthLife** — image + short-form video + auto-schedule to TikTok/IG; brand-safe lifestyle positioning. Lite $29/mo (Twitter only), Hobbyist $49/mo (20 TikTok/YT Shorts), Creator $99/mo (voice, Patreon/IG, unlimited video). **No documented persona-state vault, no AI-disclosure tooling.** Closest brand-safe near-fit — but 2 of the 4 pillars (state, disclosure) absent.
- **Creatify** — AI avatar video for UGC ads; Aurora model claims "100% message consistency across channels." Starter $19/mo, Pro $49/mo. Lightweight scheduling; no structured persona-state or disclosure audit. Ad-creative tool, not an operator OS.
- **The Influencer AI / MakeInfluencer AI** — "zero learning curve," post daily lifestyle photos of a consistent character within an hour; integrates new video models on release + monetization. Cross-platform scheduling / state depth unclear.
- **Picsart Persona + Storyline** (Mar 2026, 150M-user platform) — character identity persistence from photos; Storyline holds consistency across multi-scene sequences. Free tier + paid ~$5–10/mo. **No cross-platform scheduling, no voice layer, no disclosure.** A large platform adding exactly the consistency feature — one product sprint from the full stack.

## Tier 2 — INDIRECT (point tools operators stitch today)

- **Higgsfield + Soul ID** — Soul ID trains a persistent identity from ~15–20 photos (~$3/session); locks face/outfit/style across image gens, prevents "face drift." Soul V2 extends to video, but image→video identity carryover is **single-source / not fully confirmed**. Basic $5/mo (70 credits), Plus $39/mo annual, Ultra $99/mo. No scheduling, no voice, no disclosure. The consistency wedge is real and cheap — but confirmed image-only.
- **HeyGen** — **$100M ARR (Oct 2025), $500M valuation, ~1,024% YoY ARR growth**, profitable since Q2 2023. Avatar V: studio-grade avatar from 15-sec clip; API + native app. Creator $29/mo, Pro $99/mo, Business $149/mo+. No native cross-platform scheduling, no persona-state vault. **Most credible "ceiling" consolidator — if it adds a persona vault + scheduling (or acquires a scheduler), it collapses the OS gap from above.**
- **ElevenLabs** — voice cloning; Starter $5/mo, Creator $22/mo (~100 min). Pure point tool.
- **Kling AI** — video gen; Standard $10/mo, Pro $37/mo; API ~$0.028/s. No identity lock, no scheduler.
- **Postiz** — open-source cross-platform scheduler (20+ networks incl. IG/TikTok/X); **self-hosted free (AGPL-3.0)**; cloud $29–49/mo; 29.8k GitHub stars, has AI image/video hooks + public API. **Self-hosted $0 floors the scheduling component at zero and commoditizes the cross-platform glue.**
- **Metricool** — analytics-heavy scheduler, ~$30–100/mo; no persona/consistency features.
- **Buffer** — simplest scheduler; $6/mo/channel; free plan = 3 channels; no AI-persona features.

## Tier 3 — POTENTIAL ACQUIRERS

- **HeyGen** — $500M valuation, owns video avatar, active M&A posture (acquired Genova Labs Sep 2025); most logical consolidator.
- **Canva** — acquired Leonardo AI (Jul 2024); 200M+ users; creator-monetization push (Apr 2026).
- **Picsart** — just launched Persona/Storyline (Mar 2026) targeting the "$40B influencer economy"; 150M users; completing the stack (scheduling/voice/disclosure) is the natural next step.
- **Fanvue** — ~$200M ARR (May 2026), ~15% revenue from AI creators; already has voice cloning, analytics, AI moderation + disclosure policy; could buy brand-safe tooling to differentiate from adult-skewed incumbents.
- **ByteDance / CapCut** — deep TikTok integration, AI-avatar features; US regulatory uncertainty limits appeal.
- **Hootsuite / Buffer / Sprout** — scheduling incumbents threatened by Postiz commoditization; adding AI-persona OS is the defensive move (none has acted).

## Tier 4 — ADJACENT (could move in, 12–18 mo)

- **Picsart** — already moving (Persona/Storyline Mar 2026); missing scheduling/voice/disclosure — one sprint away.
- **Canva** — design-first, adding AI video + scheduling; persona persistence trajectory.
- **Adobe Firefly** — custom brand-style model training (Mar 2026), commercial-cleared outputs; adding persona consistency is adjacent; compliance-friendly brand fits.
- **Fanvue** — AI-creator-native; could add gen tooling to become a full persona OS from the monetization side.
- **Social schedulers (Buffer/Later/Hootsuite)** — adding AI caption/image; none has persona-state/consistency yet.

## Where the integrated-OS middle is squeezed

- **Floor:** Postiz self-hosted $0 (scheduling) + Higgsfield Basic $5 (image consistency) + ElevenLabs $5 (voice) + Kling $10 (video) ≈ **$20–40/mo in direct tool cost** for a technically-capable assembler. The "$77/mo" is partly a labor/time premium for gluing, not pure tool cost. **The price floor is real.**
- **Ceiling:** HeyGen ($100M ARR, ~1,024% YoY) is the credible consolidator from above; Picsart (150M users, Persona Mar 2026) is the fastest-moving adjacent threat. Likely closes within **12–18 months** on an incumbent move.
- **The uncovered gap (today):** no product combines (a) structured persona-state vault (voice + visual + backstory bible), (b) image→video cross-modal consistency lock, (c) cross-platform publish/schedule, AND (d) per-post AI-disclosure automation, in **one brand-safe** product. Disclosure is a genuine forcing function: FTC dual-disclosure active, NY Synthetic Performer Disclosure Law (eff. 9 Jun 2026), EU AI Act Art.50 (enforceable 2 Aug 2026), FTC fines up to ~$53K/violation. No current all-in-one bakes this in.
