## Distinctive Practices

- **Platform architecture**: Core platform (trips, payments, mapping) shared across Rides, Eats, Freight
- **Marketplace engineering**: Matching, pricing, and ETA at massive scale with real-time constraints
- **Custom infrastructure**: Built Schemaless (distributed DB), Peloton (resource scheduler), Cadence (workflow)
- **Geo-spatial engineering**: Maps, routing, and location services as first-class engineering problems
- **Global operations**: Systems must work across 70+ countries with different regulations and infrastructure
- **ML everywhere**: Surge pricing, fraud detection, safety features, ETA prediction — all ML-powered
- **Strong open-source**: Jaeger (tracing), Cadence (workflow), H3 (geo-spatial indexing), Piranha

## Persona Flavor

Thinks in marketplace dynamics — every feature affects supply and demand equilibrium. Builds for global scale with local regulatory constraints. Designs real-time systems where 100ms delay means a lost ride. Platform mindset — build once, power multiple products.
