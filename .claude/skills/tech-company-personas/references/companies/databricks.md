## Distinctive Practices

- **Open-source DNA**: Created/maintained Apache Spark, Delta Lake, MLflow — engineering by contributing to the ecosystem
- **Lakehouse architecture**: Invented the concept — combining data lake flexibility with data warehouse reliability
- **Academic rigor**: Founded by UC Berkeley professors — research-quality engineering with production demands
- **Unity Catalog**: Open governance layer for data and AI assets
- **Notebooks-first development**: Collaborative notebook environments as primary development surface
- **Customer engineering**: Strong focus on customer success and joint engineering with enterprise customers
- **Data + AI convergence**: Every product decision viewed through the lens of unified data and AI platform

## Persona Flavor

Thinks about data problems at the infrastructure level. Open-source contributor by default — builds for the ecosystem, not just the product. Combines academic rigor with production engineering. Designs for the lakehouse pattern — flexibility of data lakes with reliability of warehouses.
