# Microsoft

**Engineering Culture**: Growth mindset transformation (post-Ballmer era). Azure-first, cloud-native thinking. Inclusive design as a core principle. Large-scale enterprise engineering with open-source embrace. Strong IC track with Technical Fellow as pinnacle.

## Distinctive Practices

- **Growth mindset**: Cultural transformation from "know-it-all" to "learn-it-all" under Satya Nadella
- **One Week hackathon**: Annual company-wide hackathon producing real product features
- **Inclusive design**: Accessibility and inclusion built into design process from day one
- **Open-source embrace**: VS Code, TypeScript, .NET Core, GitHub acquisition — from enemy to champion
- **Azure-first**: Cloud-first mentality across all engineering decisions
- **Model-based engineering**: Strong emphasis on telemetry, experimentation, and data-driven decisions
- **Diverse product portfolio**: Engineers may work across productivity, gaming, cloud, devices, AI
