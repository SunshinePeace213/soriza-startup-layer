Last 12 months (May 2025 → May 2026) have a separate sub-table with extra detail.

Source tags:
- **[PRIMARY]** — Musk has stated this himself, on record (Rogan, Isaacson bio, court testimony, X posts, earnings calls)
- **[SECONDARY]** — Reported by credible third parties (NYT, WSJ, Reuters, NPR, Bloomberg, Walter Isaacson biography)
- **[INFERENCE]** — Interpretation drawn from the pattern of behavior, not from a direct statement

## Section B — Phase Descriptions

**Early Life (1971–1995)** — South African childhood marked by domestic violence, severe bullying, and his parents' divorce. Self-taught coder, voracious sci-fi reader; emigrates alone at 17 via Canadian passport, lands at UPenn. The pattern set here — exit a hostile system, out-think it, build the next one yourself — never changes.

**Founder Era I (1995–2008)** — Two consecutive software exits (Zip2, PayPal) give him ~$200M. Instead of retiring he founds SpaceX (2002) and joins Tesla (2004), then bets every cent on both. Ends with the legendary December 2008 brink — Falcon 1 reaches orbit, NASA contract lands, last personal cash splits between two companies on Christmas Eve.

**Bet-the-Company Era (2008–2018)** — Decade of operational scaling: Tesla IPO, Model S, Falcon 9 booster landing, Master Plans 1 & 2, ISS docking, Solar City, OpenAI, Neuralink, Boring Co. Ends with the 2018 trifecta — "pedo guy," "funding secured," Rogan joint — that publicly fuses CEO and personality and starts his slow alienation from mainstream institutions.

**Cultural Figure Era (2018–2022)** — Pandemic-era posture: anti-lockdown, anti-California, pro-Bitcoin, pro-meme. Tesla joins S&P 500, becomes world's richest person, hosts SNL, becomes *Time* Person of the Year. Personal life (Grimes, Zilis, Vivian's transition) bleeds into political identity.

**Owner-of-Twitter Era (2022–2024)** — Twitter acquisition + mass layoffs + xAI founding + X rebrand. Public posting becomes 8+ hours/day. Advertisers, regulators, and old allies break away. Endorses Trump after the Butler assassination attempt and spends ~$300M on his campaign.

**Political-Power Era (2024–present)** — Leads DOGE inside the Trump administration, becomes briefly the most powerful unelected figure in the US, then breaks publicly with Trump over the One Big Beautiful Bill, announces "America Party," reconciles months later at Kirk's memorial. SpaceX files for $1.75T IPO that would make him history's first trillionaire. Tesla narrative shifts from "EV company" to "Optimus + robotaxi or bust." xAI Colossus 2 races OpenAI/Anthropic.
