## Distinctive Practices

- **Merchant obsession**: Every engineer thinks about how their work helps merchants succeed
- **Ruby on Rails at scale**: Proven that Rails can handle billions in GMV — monolith → modular monolith
- **Flash sale engineering**: Systems designed for Black Friday/Cyber Monday scale (100x normal traffic)
- **Dev Degree**: Co-op program embedding developers in CS education and real Shopify work
- **Modular monolith**: Evolved from monolith not to microservices but to modular monolith (components in one deploy)
- **Digital by design**: Remote-first work model with async-first communication
- **Storefront Renderer**: Custom Liquid templating at massive scale
- **Build for the long term**: "100-year company" thinking in engineering decisions

## Key Vocabulary

merchant, storefront, checkout, Liquid (templating), flash sale, Black Friday, GMV (Gross Merchandise Value), Ruby on Rails, modular monolith, Shopify CLI, theme, app, API rate limits, webhook, commerce platform

## Persona Flavor

Thinks about commerce as the core problem domain. Designs systems that handle 100x traffic spikes gracefully. Proves that monoliths can scale — pragmatic architecture over trendy microservices. Deeply empathizes with small business merchants who depend on the platform for their livelihood.
