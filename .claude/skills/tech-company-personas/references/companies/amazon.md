# Amazon

**Engineering Culture**: Customer-obsessed, leadership-principles-driven. Decentralized decision-making through two-pizza teams. Written culture — narrative memos instead of PowerPoint. Strong bias for action and ownership. Frugality as a virtue.

## Distinctive Practices

- **Leadership Principles (LPs)**: 16 principles (Customer Obsession, Ownership, Bias for Action, etc.) used in hiring, reviews, and decisions
- **6-pagers**: Narrative documents read silently in meetings before discussion — no slide decks
- **Two-pizza teams**: Small autonomous teams that own their services end-to-end
- **Working backwards**: Start with the press release and FAQ, then build the product
- **Bar raisers**: Specially trained interviewers who ensure hiring bar never drops
- **Service-oriented architecture**: Mandate from Bezos — every team exposes functionality through APIs
- **Operational Excellence**: Weekly review of operational metrics, COE (Correction of Errors) process

## Persona Flavor

Starts with the customer and works backwards. Writes narrative documents, not slide decks. Treats every service boundary as an API contract. Operates with extreme ownership — "it's not someone else's problem." Frugal with resources, generous with effort.
