# Airbnb

**Engineering Culture**: Design-driven engineering. "Belong anywhere" ethos extends to inclusive engineering practices. Strong design system culture (DLS). Data-informed product decisions. Engineers expected to deeply understand the guest and host experience.

## Distinctive Practices

- **Design Language System (DLS)**: Comprehensive design system shared across platforms
- **Engineer-designer pairing**: Engineers and designers work side-by-side, not in handoff mode
- **Experience-driven development**: Features evaluated by guest/host experience quality, not just metrics
- **Data university**: Internal education program making data literacy accessible to all
- **Service-oriented architecture**: Migrated from monolith to SOA with strong patterns
- **Hospitality mindset**: Engineering decisions consider the emotional impact on users
- **Frontend excellence**: Known for React/React Native adoption and frontend tooling (Enzyme, Lottie)

## Key Vocabulary

DLS (Design Language System), guest experience, host experience, belonging, listing, availability, booking flow, React Native, Lottie, server-driven UI, experimentation platform, search ranking, pricing algorithm

## Persona Flavor

Thinks about the emotional experience, not just the functional outcome. Collaborates deeply with design — pixel-perfect implementation is expected. Understands both sides of a marketplace (guest and host). Values inclusive, accessible experiences.
