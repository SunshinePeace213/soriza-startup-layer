## Intellectual Evolution Markers

- **1977–85 (Foster City childhood/teens):** Tolkien + math + chess + libertarianism. Outsider-immigrant self-conception. The names Palantir, Mithril, Valar, Anduril, Rivendell, Arda all begin here, decades before they become company names.
- **1985–89 (Stanford undergrad):** Three formative shocks: (1) replacement of Western Culture program → founds *Stanford Review* in 1987 → builds the network (Sacks, Howery, later Nosek, later still Vance via Yale law). (2) Discovers **René Girard** and mimetic theory in his class. (3) Begins reading **Leo Strauss**.
- **1989–96 (Law/finance interlude):** Stanford Law (1989–92) → 11th Circuit clerkship → Sullivan & Cromwell (~7 months, hated it) → Credit Suisse derivatives + Bennett speechwriting (1993–96). The "anti-credentialism" of the Thiel Fellowship is partly his own confession. *The Diversity Myth* (1995) is the artifact of this period.
- **1996–2002 (Operator phase):** Thiel Capital (1996) → Confinity (1998) → PayPal merger (2000) → CEO via Musk ouster (2000) → IPO (2002) → eBay sale (2002). Forms the "PayPal Mafia." Tests his contrarian/monopoly instincts in practice.
- **2002–07 (Investor + macro phase):** Clarium (2002), Palantir (2003), Facebook (2004), Founders Fund (2005). Macro view sharpens around dollar/oil. Identity still officially libertarian, but the Straussian/Schmittian/Girardian synthesis is being written ("The Straussian Moment," 2007).
- **2007–11 (Libertarian apex + exit):** Outed by Gawker (Dec 2007). Endorses Ron Paul (Dec 2007). Funds Seasteading Institute (Apr 2008). Writes "Education of a Libertarian" (Apr 2009). Secures NZ citizenship (2011). Apex exit-not-voice phase. Then Clarium collapses (2008–11), forcing him to admit macro/political prediction is hard.
- **2011–16 (Founder ideology phase):** Thiel Fellowship (2010–11), CS183 at Stanford (2012), *Zero to One* and "Competition Is for Losers" (2014). Anti-competition, pro-monopoly, anti-stagnation. Politics recedes; founder-mythology dominates.
- **2016–22 (Voice phase / counter-elite):** Bollea/Gawker secret funding revealed (May 2016). RNC speech (Jul 2016). Trump transition (Nov 2016). Out of closet publicly, married (2017). LA move (2018). Vance introduction to Trump (2021). Meta board exit (Feb 2022). $32M into 2022 midterms.
- **2022–24 (Strategic pause):** Withdraws from direct candidate giving after 2022. Lets the network (Vance, Sacks, Mikolay, Kratsios, etc.) do the work. Begins talking Antichrist publicly.
- **2024–26 (Religious / civilizational phase):** Joe Rogan (Aug 2024). FT op-ed (Jan 2025). ARC Forum (Feb 2025). Jordan Peterson (Apr 2025). Ross Douthat NYT podcast (Jun 2025). Antichrist lecture series leaked (Sep–Oct 2025). Rome lectures (Mar 2026). The frame is now apocalyptic-Christian. Investments accelerate (Anduril $1B, Panthalassa $140M, Founders Fund Growth IV $6B). Wealth ~$28B on Palantir AI boom.

## Last 12 Months (2025-05 → 2026-05) — Mandatory Section

**Public talks / writing:**
- **Jun 26 2025 — NYT "Interesting Times" with Ross Douthat:** Three-part conversation. Calls Greta Thunberg the Antichrist not metaphorically; argues biblical apocalypse should be read literally. Restates stagnation thesis: AI is "more than a nothing burger but less than the total transformation of our society"; ~1 pp/year GDP for a decade.
