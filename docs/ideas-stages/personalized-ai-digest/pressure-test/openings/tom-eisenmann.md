# Tom Eisenmann — Opening

## Verdict
This reads like a False Start dressed as a problem — confidently specified, entirely unvalidated.

## Objections
1. The whole hypothesis rests on a single-cause story: people stay overloaded *because* digests aren't personalized. That's the loss of blood, not the gunshot wound. I'd want to know the actual cause before I'd build. Maybe they over-follow because following 20 creators is identity and FOMO, not job need — in which case a tighter filter doesn't change the behavior at all.

2. Your status-quo is "free." TLDR AI and Ben's Bites already own the inbox and the habit. Personalization-plus-skill-calibration is a feature, not a Diamond — I don't yet see the technology moat, the profit formula, or why an incumbent with distribution can't bolt on a "skill level" toggle in a week. Validate willingness-to-pay against free before you write code.
